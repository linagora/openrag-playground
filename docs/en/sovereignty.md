# Data Sovereignty

**OpenRAG Playground** is designed with European digital sovereignty at its core.

**Self-hosted.** The entire application runs on your infrastructure. No cloud dependency, no SaaS lock-in.

**Zero external requests.** The application makes no calls to any external service. All assets are vendored locally. Your browser communicates only with your server.

**Open source.** The full source code is available under AGPL-3.0, ensuring transparency, auditability, and freedom from vendor lock-in.

**European provider.** Built by [LINAGORA](https://linagora.com), headquartered in Toulouse, France. Subject to European law and GDPR.

**Your data stays yours.** Documents, tokens, and configuration never leave your infrastructure. No telemetry, no data sharing, no third-party processing.
